Our Story

A legacy of craftsmanship rooted in the heart of North Carolina

Donald Herbin has been cutting, polishing, and crafting gemstones for over a decade in Greensboro, North Carolina. What started as a passion for the earth's natural treasures has grown into Southeastern Lapidary Associates — a trusted source for NC native stones, custom cuts, wire-wrapped jewelry, and hands-on lapidary education. Every stone tells a story, and Donald brings that story to life with master craftsmanship and fair prices.

North Carolina Gemstones

Our state is rich in world-class gemstone deposits

Ruby

Cowee Valley rubies are world-renowned. From deep pigeon blood to pink, NC rubies rival those found anywhere on Earth.

Sapphire

Franklin and Cowee Valley produce stunning blue, pink, and star sapphires prized by collectors worldwide.

Emerald

Hiddenite, NC is one of the only places in North America where gem-quality emeralds are found naturally.

Garnet

Rich rhodolite and almandine garnets are abundant in the NC mountains, offering deep reds and purples.

Aquamarine

Spruce Pine and the Blue Ridge produce exceptional aquamarine crystals with vivid sea-blue color.

Smoky Quartz

Abundant throughout the NC Appalachians, smoky quartz ranges from light tea to deep chocolate brown.
